HyperText Today: Impact and Future Direction:

Current Role:

HyperText is also used in fields such as knowledge management, information visualization, and data analysis to represent complex systems and relationships in an interactive and easy-to-understand way. The impact of HyperText on the way that information is organized and accessed cannot be overstated.
